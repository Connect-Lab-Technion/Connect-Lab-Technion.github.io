Solomon Goldgraber-Casspi is now officially a PhD student after passing is candidacy exam. Solomon presented his research proposal on an input-output perspective of formation control problems.  His ideas will unify new concepts from higher-order rigidity theory with ideas from controllability of non-linear systems.  Exciting times!  Congratulations!
