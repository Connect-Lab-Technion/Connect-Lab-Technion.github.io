We are pleased to share that Zamir Martinez presented his work at the [IAAC Graduate Students in Systems and Control (GSC) Workshop](https://iaac.technion.ac.il/workshops/2026/2026-4-27-inv.pdf). The GSC is the annual meeting of graduate students in Israel working in systems and control, offering an opportunity to present research and build connections within the community.

![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/55233709615_0c7c2c7f3c_b.jpg)

Zamir’s presentation was based on his recent paper accepted to the American Control Conference (ACC 2026). The work develops an edge-based framework for controlling networked systems, where control objectives are formulated on edge variables and then realized at the level of the agents.

A preprint is available here: [ACC 2026 paper](https://connect-lab-technion.github.io/Publications/Martinez_ACC26.pdf).
